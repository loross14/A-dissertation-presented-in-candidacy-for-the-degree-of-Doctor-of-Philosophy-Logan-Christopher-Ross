## AFK Scan

Start with **04 Planisphere Scroll**, **01 Engraved Scroll Prime**, and **02 Wax-Sealed Scroll**. Those are the strongest candidates for the actual public mark because they preserve the copper-scroll idea while still feeling like a game artifact and Logan identity mark.

Favicon rule: full `L.C. Ross` usually cannot survive at 32px. The small mark should simplify to a durable silhouette plus `LC`/`LCR` identity, while the 116px homepage mark and social mark preserve `L.C. Ross`.

## Twenty Options

1. **Engraved Scroll Prime** - Communicates handcrafted copper scroll, direct continuity with the current mark, and clear `L.C. Ross` ownership. Strength: safest public evolution and strong parent/sibling readability. Risk: least radical of the set.
2. **Wax-Sealed Scroll** - Communicates authorship, secrecy, and an impressed artifact. Strength: memorable and warm, with game-object energy. Risk: red wax could feel more medieval than decipherment if pushed too hard.
3. **Four-Door Tablet** - Communicates the four locked rooms at first glance. Strength: best conceptual match to the homepage game. Risk: more board-game emblem than personal mark.
4. **Planisphere Scroll** - Communicates witness geometry plus copper identity. Strength: best bridge between site, seal, record, and game. Risk: more visually complex at favicon scale.
5. **Inscription Eye** - Communicates looking, reading, and being read by the puzzle. Strength: mysterious and distinctive. Risk: could feel too occult if not restrained.
6. **Record Rosette** - Communicates stamped record and durable authorship. Strength: solid social/identity mark. Risk: can drift bureaucratic if over-labeled.
7. **Octagonal Game Token** - Communicates collectible game artifact without blockchain-first cues. Strength: strong silhouette and badge utility. Risk: less obviously a scroll.
8. **Shadow-Mirror Split** - Communicates the doctrine directly through a split field. Strength: site-specific and intellectually clear. Risk: side labels only work large.
9. **Whale Cadence Scroll** - Communicates the final boss room through wave cadence. Strength: brings the fourth room into the identity. Risk: could over-index on Whale Cadence versus the whole hub.
10. **Rongorongo Reed** - Communicates decipherment texture and repeated marks. Strength: pattern-first feel. Risk: script texture may get busy at small sizes.
11. **Proto-Ledger Plate** - Communicates accounting structure and method. Strength: intellectually precise. Risk: colder and less magical.
12. **Indus Seal Square** - Communicates seal culture, four pressure points, and compactness. Strength: favicon-stable. Risk: square seal can feel less personal unless the nameplate is prominent.
13. **Witness Aperture** - Communicates instrument, witness, and geometry. Strength: elegant social mark. Risk: least parent-readable as "copper scroll."
14. **Keyhole Door** - Communicates locked rooms instantly. Strength: strongest game metaphor. Risk: identity text is subordinate to the keyhole.
15. **Copper Compass** - Communicates method-finding and navigation. Strength: strong silhouette. Risk: more expedition than decipherment.
16. **Folded Map Scroll** - Communicates routes through game, essays, and records. Strength: approachable and flexible. Risk: folded-map panels can get delicate.
17. **Archive Ticket** - Communicates access pass, record, and Room 137. Strength: friendly and understandable. Risk: less iconic as a favicon.
18. **Night Planisphere** - Communicates celestial instrument and mystery. Strength: sophisticated dark-field identity. Risk: star details disappear small.
19. **Hammered Copper Coin** - Communicates tactile artifact and permanence. Strength: easiest small-size mark. Risk: coin language may feel too generic unless the name engraving is excellent.
20. **Library Spine** - Communicates essays, volume, and authorship. Strength: clearest stacked `L.C.` / `Ross` identity at small sizes. Risk: weaker fit for the four-room game.

## Top 3 Recommendation

1. **04 Planisphere Scroll** - Best total-system identity: copper scroll, witness geometry, record, and game all present.
2. **01 Engraved Scroll Prime** - Best conservative production successor to the current favicon and homepage mark.
3. **02 Wax-Sealed Scroll** - Best emotional artifact: memorable, warm, and clearly made by/for Logan.

## Small-Size Rule

At favicon size, the strongest designs should simplify to a silhouette plus an `LC`/`LCR` cue rather than forcing the full `L.C. Ross` inscription. At 116px homepage scale, the chosen mark should preserve `L.C. Ross` or the stacked equivalent `L.C.` / `Ross`.
