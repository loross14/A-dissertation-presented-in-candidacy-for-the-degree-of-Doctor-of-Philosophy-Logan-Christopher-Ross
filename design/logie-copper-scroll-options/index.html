Twenty L.C. Ross copper mark directions

Each option is shown as a favicon-sized mark, the 116px homepage hero mark, and a larger social/identity mark. The full L.C. Ross inscription is preserved at hero scale unless the option intentionally stacks it as L.C. / Ross.

Scratch-only preview. Production favicon, homepage asset, public routing, public/index.html, vercel.json, and public/logie-copper-scroll.svg are not edited by this chooser.

Top 3 to inspect first

  1. 04 Planisphere Scroll - best total-system bridge: copper scroll, witness geometry, record, and game.
  2. 01 Engraved Scroll Prime - safest successor to the current public mark.

Small-size rule

At favicon size, full L.C. Ross usually becomes texture. Choose a silhouette that survives at 32px, then keep the full name legible at the 116px homepage mark and larger social mark.
